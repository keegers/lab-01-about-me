Cinema 4d

Background Information

You've probably seen something made in Cinema 4d and not realized it. 3d renders are getting closer and closer to photorealism and there are multiple programs to keep an eye on. One of the quickest tools to learn and get up and running in 3D land is Cinema 4d. It's been around for a while, but because of that it has a strong community. It also has various 3rd party plugins and pipelines to 3rd party software that can allow you to plug it into your pipeline at some point. Product designers like tools like Cinema 4d because you can use scanned assets and at the same time create environments and animations in 3d scenes. Even some movies have been used VFX created in Cinema 4d. These include: The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, Inception, and Tron: Legacy.

Related Software

  • Houdini is frequently used with Cinema 4d to generate procedural animations and to create VDB volumes.

  • Unreal is becoming more important for Cinema 4d worflow thanks to Quixel and various import and export pipelines.

  • After Effects is one of the go to compositing tools for Cinema 4d. It is also not well known that Cinema 4D Lite is available through After Effects.
